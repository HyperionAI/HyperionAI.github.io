How It Works
Sign Up and Get the App
Our free app is available on iOS and Android, and it’s useful whether or not you choose to subscribe to a nutritional plan. You can track fitness and diet goals, control programmable reminders and push notifications, and even keep daily notes about how you are feeling to help you stay on track.
Connect Your Devices
Hyperion.AI can automatically import data from your fitbit or smart scale, giving you the power to analyze all your health data in one place and make smart choices about diet and excercise changes.
Activate a Supplement Subscription
Hyperion.AI can use the data profile from your account to suggest a good starting supplement to meet your needs, which will be refined over time as your habits and body change.
The Adaptive Neural Network
Hyperion.AI is powered by a neural network capable of using your data to construct an accurate supplement profile for you. As time goes on, the network can interpret your feedback and adapt to meet your changing needs. For more information on neural networks and what makes Hyperion.AI so powerful, see our FAQ
