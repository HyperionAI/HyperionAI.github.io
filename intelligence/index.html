Artificial Intelligence
Thinking about your health. 24/7.
Hyperion.AI uses dozens of potential ‘neurons’ as data inputs. When you set up your profile, you can tell Hyperion which of the neurons you want to activate. For example, if you want to integrate data from your Fitbit Flex, a neuron to input data about how many calories you burned each day will be activated. If you choose to integrate data from a smart scale, a neuron evaluating your progress towards your goal weight will be activated. In addition to the passive App Integration Neurons, there are Active Input Neurons. These neurons collect data from you directly, asking you questions periodically to determine how good you feel, how much energy you have, etc. Together these neurons construct a data profile for your individual needs on a monthly basis, to help determine the composition of your next month’s batch of supplements. The data is then combined mathematically and sent to several ‘hidden layers.’ This is where the learning takes place. When the Hyperion was first created, the hidden variables would perform an arbitrary sigmoidal function on the data to achieve a result. Since the function was arbitrary, the result was imperfect. Hyperion used the error of the first result to refine the hidden layer function, and tried again. The second result was better, but still not perfect. After over 100,000 tries, Hyperion has evolved some sophisticated weights and algorithms to give nearly perfect results in every data iteration…and it still continues to learn and improve. As a result we now have a powerful program to determine your exact nutritional needs based upon your data, allowing us to customize your supplement mix every month to help you be at your best.
